PersistentVolume (PV) is a piece of storage in the cluster 
that has been provisioned by an administrator or dynamically provisioned using StorageClasses. 
It is independent of any individual pod and remains even after the pod is deleted, 
allowing data to persist across pod restarts or rescheduling.
